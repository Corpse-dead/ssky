About SSKY

At SSKY, we're more than just a brand — we're a continuation of a half-century legacy built on ink, passion, and precision. Rooted in the proud heritage of Sumeet Printers, a trusted name in the printing industry since the era of the letterpress, our journey has spanned generations, evolving from traditional techniques to cutting-edge digital craftsmanship. What started decades ago with the rhythmic clatter of metal type has now transformed into a dynamic hub for premium posters, stickers, postcards, and other eye-catching paper goods — designed for modern tastes and vibrant spaces. Our name, SSKY, reflects the limitless possibilities of print — sky-high in quality, impact, and imagination.
