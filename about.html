About SSKY Printing

A half-century legacy of ink, passion, and precision — from letterpress to digital craftsmanship

Our Legacy

At SSKY, we're more than just a brand — we're a continuation of a half-century legacy built on ink, passion, and precision. Rooted in the proud heritage of Sumeet Printers, a trusted name in the printing industry since the era of the letterpress, our journey has spanned generations.

What started decades ago with the rhythmic clatter of metal type has now transformed into a dynamic hub for premium posters, stickers, postcards, and other eye-catching paper goods — designed for modern tastes and vibrant spaces.

Evolution & Innovation

Our journey has evolved from traditional techniques to cutting-edge digital craftsmanship. Whether it's a motivational wall piece, a vintage car poster, or a set of aesthetic desk postcards, we bring print to life with bold creativity and unmatched quality.

From bedrooms to boardrooms, SSKY adds a touch of personality and inspiration to every wall, desk, or diary. With our in-house production backed by decades of mastery, we ensure every piece you hold is a blend of history, innovation, and style.

Our Vision

Our name, SSKY, reflects the limitless possibilities of print — sky-high in quality, impact, and imagination. Every product we offer is printed with care, reflecting not just trends, but stories. And behind every print is the expertise of a company that's seen the art of printing evolve — and helped shape it.
